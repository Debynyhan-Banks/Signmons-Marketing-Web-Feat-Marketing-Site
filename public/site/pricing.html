FAQ

Common Questions

Is there a free trial? ▾
Yes — every plan comes with a 14-day free trial, no credit card required. Test everything including live call handling and payment collection before committing.
Can I cancel anytime? ▾
Absolutely. Month-to-month plans cancel with one click, no questions asked. Annual plans can be cancelled within the first 30 days for a full refund.
What if I go over 100 calls on Starter? ▾
We'll notify you and offer a one-click upgrade to Growth. We never drop calls — your customers will always be answered.
Does Signmons work with my existing number? ▾
Yes. Forward your existing business number to Signmons in 2 minutes, or get a brand-new local number from us.
What payment processors do you support? ▾
Signmons integrates with Stripe, Square, and PayPal. Funds go directly to your account — we never hold your money.
