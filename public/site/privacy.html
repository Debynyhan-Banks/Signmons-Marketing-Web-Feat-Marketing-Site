1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Business name, contact information, and account credentials
  • Payment information (processed and stored by Stripe — we never store raw card numbers)
  • Phone numbers and communication preferences
  • Custom AI scripts and business configuration settings

Information Collected Automatically

  • Call logs, transcripts, and recordings from AI interactions
  • Appointment and booking data
  • Usage data, feature interactions, and session analytics
  • Device type, browser, IP address, and general location

Customer Data

When your customers interact with Signmons AI on your behalf, we collect their name, phone number, service request details, and payment info. You are the data controller — we process it only at your direction.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use information we collect to provide and improve the platform, process payments, train AI models using anonymized data only, send transactional notifications, provide support, comply with legal obligations, and detect fraud. We do not use your data for targeted advertising.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share information only with: service providers (Stripe, Twilio, AWS — all bound by data processing agreements), legal compliance (when required by law or court order), and business transfers (we will notify you in advance of any merger or acquisition).

4. Data Retention

We retain your data while your account is active. After cancellation, data is kept for 30 days to allow export, then permanently deleted — except payment records retained 7 years per IRS requirements.

5. Security

We implement AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.3 for data in transit, SOC 2 Type II compliant infrastructure, regular third-party security audits, and role-based access controls. Contact security@signmons.com if you believe your account has been compromised.

6. Your Rights

👁️

Access

Request a full copy of the data we hold about you at any time.

✏️

Correction

Update or correct inaccurate information directly in your account or by contacting us.

🗑️

Deletion

Request complete deletion of your account and data. We honor this within 30 days.

📦

Portability

Export all your data (calls, bookings, customers) in CSV format from your dashboard.

🚫

Opt-Out

Opt out of AI model training using your data at any time in account settings.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@signmons.com.

7. Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management, and privacy-preserving analytics to understand platform usage. No third-party ad tracking. You can disable non-essential cookies in your browser settings.

8. Children's Privacy

Signmons is intended for adults 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Contact us immediately if you believe a child has provided us with personal information.

9. Policy Changes

We will notify you of material changes via email and in-app notification at least 14 days before they take effect. Continued use after that date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
